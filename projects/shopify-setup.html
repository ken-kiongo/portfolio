Overview

The client needed a complete Shopify store built from the ground up. Starting from theme selection and customization, the project covered every aspect of the store — products, collections, navigation, checkout, payments, and shipping — all the way through to launch.

Key focus areas included store speed, clean product organization, and integrating apps that would reduce manual work for the store owner on an ongoing basis.

Deliverables

  • Product catalog setup and organization
  • Collections and navigation structure
  • Payment gateway configuration
  • Domestic and international shipping rules
  • Essential app integrations
  • Store performance optimization
  • Pre-launch QA and testing
